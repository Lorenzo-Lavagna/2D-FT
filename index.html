2D-FT

2D inverse Fourier Transform real-time editor online. Simple and phone-friendly; you can input photos directly from your camera. Click or touch the spectrum to edit the mask and see the result of the inverse transform.
This project is based on the original work of Tetsuro Sakamoto: 2D Inverse Fourier Transform Playground. Thanks Tetsuro!
